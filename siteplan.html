WDD 131 - SITE PLAN DOCUMENT

Summary

The site plan document is a planning document that outlines the site's purpose, target audience, content, and other important aspects of the website. The document is used to guide the development of the website and ensure that the site meets the needs of the target audience. The site plan document is an essential part of the website development process and should be created before any design or development work begins.

Typography

Roboto will be the primary font for the Engineer Library Website. Its versatile, modern design ensures clarity and legibility on screens of all sizes, suitable for both headings and body text. Available in various weights and styles, it offers flexibility in design and layout.

Wireframes

The wireframes for the Engineer Library Website are designed to provide a visual representation of the layout and structure of the site. The wireframes show the key elements of the site, including the homepage, report list, report details, and call log pages. The wireframes are designed to be simple and easy to understand, providing a clear overview of the site's content and functionality..
